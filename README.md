# 🚖 Uber Data Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)<br>

## 📊 Project Overview<br>  
This project presents an **interactive Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze Uber ride data and generate meaningful business insights.<br>  
It helps in understanding **customer behavior, revenue trends, booking patterns, and vehicle performance** through dynamic visualizations.<br><br>

---

## 🎯 Key Objectives<br>  
- Analyze **total bookings, revenue, and distance traveled**<br>  
- Track **customer and driver ratings**<br>  
- Identify **high-performing vehicle types**<br>  
- Monitor **monthly trends and booking patterns**<br>  
- Evaluate **payment methods and customer distribution**<br><br>

---

## 📌 Dashboard Features<br>

### 🏠 1. Overview Page<br>  
- **KPIs**:<br>  
  - Total Bookings<br>  
  - Lost Bookings<br>  
  - Total Revenue<br>  
  - Total Distance<br>  
  - Average Distance<br>  
- Monthly booking trend (Line Chart)<br>  
- Revenue by vehicle type<br>  
- Booking value by month<br>  
- Lost bookings analysis<br>  
- Customer & driver ratings<br><br>

---

### 🚗 2. Vehicle Analysis Page<br>  
- Revenue by vehicle type<br>  
- Customer count by vehicle<br>  
- Completed bookings per vehicle<br>  
- Monthly booking trends (Sparklines)<br>  
- Comparative analysis of vehicle performance<br><br>

---

### 💰 3. Revenue Analysis Page<br>  
- Monthly revenue trends<br>  
- Revenue by payment method (**UPI, Cash, Wallet, Card**)<br>  
- Revenue contribution by vehicle type<br>  
- Top customers based on revenue<br><br>

---

### 👥 4. Customer Analysis Page<br>  
- Customer count by month<br>  
- Customer distribution by payment method<br>  
- Customer segmentation by vehicle type<br>  
- Detailed customer-level data (Table view)<br><br>

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies<br>  
- **Power BI** – Dashboard creation & data visualization<br>  
- **Excel** – Data source<br>  
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Calculations & measures<br>  
- **Data Modeling** – Relationships & transformations<br><br>

---

## 📈 Key Insights<br>  
- **Auto and Bike** contribute the highest revenue<br>  
- **UPI** is the most preferred payment method<br>  
- Monthly booking trends show **seasonal fluctuations**<br>  
- High customer ratings indicate **good service quality**<br>  
- Lost bookings highlight **areas for improvement**<br><br>
